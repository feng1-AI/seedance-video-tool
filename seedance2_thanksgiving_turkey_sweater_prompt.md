# Seedance 2.0 Video Prompt - Thanksgiving Turkey Pet Sweater

- Clothing reference, back graphic: `seedance_assets/clothing_back_graphic.JPG`

## Main Prompt

Create a bright, clear, realistic pet apparel product video using Seedance 2.0. The dog is a small white Maltese dog, matching the provided dog model reference. The dog wears the exact Thanksgiving turkey crewneck pet sweater from the clothing reference during the entire video. Preserve the sweater exactly: beige ribbed knit fabric, crewneck shape, sleeve length, hem, fit, color, texture, and the back Thanksgiving turkey embroidery/graphic must remain unchanged, not redesigned, not recolored, not replaced, not distorted.

Scene style: clean American home and simple outdoor yard, bright natural light, sharp focus, soft realistic shadows, premium pet product commercial style. Indoor scene includes a simple sofa, warm wooden floor, neutral wall, uncluttered background. Outdoor scene includes a neat green lawn suitable for a small dog. No on-screen text, no captions, no subtitles, no logos, no watermarks.

Owner: an American white woman around 30 years old, natural everyday look, no heavy makeup, warm and friendly expression, casual simple clothing. She interacts affectionately with the dog: gentle petting, kneeling beside the dog, smiling, calling the dog over, giving a soft cuddle. All spoken dialogue and voiceover must be female English with an American accent.

Audio: cheerful lively instrumental music at low volume, lower than the natural environment sounds and voice. Include subtle environment sounds such as paws on wooden floor, light fabric movement, soft dog panting, gentle outdoor ambience.

## Shot Plan

Duration: 12-15 seconds, vertical 9:16 or horizontal 16:9 depending on platform need. Smooth camera movement, realistic product-focused pacing.

1. Indoor warm opening, medium shot. The Maltese dog walks across a wooden floor toward the owner while wearing the beige Thanksgiving turkey crewneck sweater. The owner kneels beside a simple sofa and smiles naturally.

2. Close-up of the front fit. Camera gently pushes in on the sweater collar, ribbed knit texture, sleeves, and comfortable fit around the dog's chest. The owner lightly smooths the sleeve cuff with one hand. The dog stays relaxed and happy.

3. Side/back showcase. The dog turns slightly beside the owner, showing the back of the sweater clearly. Keep the original turkey graphic and lettering exactly consistent with the reference image. The owner gently strokes the dog's back without covering the graphic.

4. Affection moment. The owner gives the dog a soft cuddle near the sofa. The dog looks up at her, tail relaxed, playful and comfortable. Keep the sweater visible and unchanged.

5. Outdoor lawn ending. The dog trots a few steps on a neat sunny lawn, then pauses in a three-quarter back view so the sweater back graphic is visible. The owner walks beside the dog and bends down to pet it. Bright, clean, cheerful ending.

## Female American Voiceover / Dialogue

Use a natural, friendly female American voice. No subtitles.

"Come here, sweetie."

"This little Thanksgiving sweater is soft, cozy, and made for every family moment."

"Look at that adorable turkey detail."

"Ready for the holiday, buddy?"

## Negative Prompt

No text, no subtitles, no captions, no watermarks, no logo overlays. Do not alter the sweater color, cut, fabric, rib texture, collar, sleeves, hem, or back graphic. Do not invent a new turkey pattern. Do not change the Thanksgiving lettering. Do not place the clothing separately; the sweater must remain worn by the dog during the entire video. No heavy makeup on the owner. No non-American accent. No dark lighting, no cluttered background, no blurry focus, no shaky camera, no surreal style, no cartoon style, no extra dogs, no cats, no duplicated limbs, no deformed paws, no distorted face, no warped embroidery.
